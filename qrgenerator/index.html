URL QR Code Generator

e.g www.example.com or https://example.com

How Use URL QR Generator

  1. Input name website on URL Field
  2. Type eg "www.examples.com" or "https://examples.com"
  3. Then click generate and click download on jpeg or pdf format

Contact QR Code Generator

How Use Contact QR Generator

  1. Input name, phone number and email on form field
  2. Then click generate and click download on jpeg or pdf format

Generate Map QR Code

How Use Map QR Generator

  1. Open google maps app
  2. Find your location
  3. Share your location using link from google map apps
  4. Copy your link location
  5. Input link from google map app to map link field
  6. Then click generate and click download on jpeg or pdf format
